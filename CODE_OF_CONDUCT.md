# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Cadenza should be a practical, respectful place to collaborate.

Examples of welcome behavior:

- Be kind, direct, and specific.
- Assume good intent, while still naming concrete problems.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Respect maintainers' decisions when tradeoffs are explained.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained derailing.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Pushing unsafe changes after maintainers have explained the risk.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, and other contributions that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may lead to a temporary or permanent ban from project spaces.

For sensitive conduct concerns, contact the maintainers privately through GitHub.
